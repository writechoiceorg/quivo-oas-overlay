# About Quivo

Founded in 2017, Quivo is a global e-commerce fulfillment platform that makes logistics smarter, faster, and more scalable. Born from the merger of LOGSTA and ANCLA with its brand PackAngels, Quivo combines operational excellence with innovative technology to simplify fulfillment and shop integrations. Today, Quivo supports over 1,500 brands worldwide.

## What We Offer

Quivo provides a comprehensive 3PL (Third-Party Logistics) solution with 6 fulfillment centers across Germany, Austria, UK, France, and the United States, plus a strategic partnership with GWC Group extending our network to the Gulf region (Qatar, Saudi Arabia, UAE, Bahrain, and Oman).

Our platform manages the complete order lifecycle—from inventory management to doorstep delivery—through:

- **The Quivo Connector**: 360° fulfillment software with 40+ shop system integrations (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, Amazon FBM, TikTok, Wix, Shopware, PrestaShop, and more) and an open API for custom integrations
- **Same-Day Fulfillment**: Process 100% of orders received before 12 PM on the same day (99.6% accuracy, 99.8% same-day fulfillment, 98% next-day delivery)
- **Multi-Carrier Shipping**: Support for major providers (Royal Mail, DHL, DPD, FedEx) with flexible shipping options and real-time tracking
- **Advanced Returns Management**: ABC classification, photographic documentation, and tailored return procedures
- **Real-time Synchronization**: Automatic stock updates across all systems and markets with live warehouse tracking
- **Advanced Features**: LOT tracking, expiry date management, batch and serial number tracking, automated customs clearance, and organic certified storage
